# Privacy Policy — SpendZ

**Last updated:** June 29, 2026  
**Contact:** hello@cyfur.in

## Overview

SpendZ ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal expense tracking application. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data we collect

### Stored on your device (not uploaded by default)

- Transaction names, amounts, dates, and types (income/expense)
- Custom categories
- App settings (e.g. biometric unlock preference)
- Backup files you create manually

This data remains on your device unless you export a backup or share it yourself.

### Collected automatically for app stability

When Firebase Crashlytics is enabled, anonymous crash reports may be sent to Google Firebase, including:

- Device model and OS version
- App version
- Stack traces when the app crashes

We do **not** intentionally include your transaction details in crash reports.

### Firebase Remote Config

The app may fetch configuration values (minimum app version, update messages) from Firebase Remote Config. No personal financial data is sent in this request.

## Data we do not collect

- We do not require an account
- We do not sell your data
- We do not upload your transactions to our servers by default
- We do not use advertising trackers

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Biometric | Unlock the app |
| Storage / Files | Export and restore backup files |
| Internet | Crash reporting, update checks, opening legal links |

## Your choices

- **Delete data:** Settings → Erase all Data
- **Export data:** Settings → Backup & Restore
- **Disable biometrics:** Settings → Biometric unlock

## Children's privacy

SpendZ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
